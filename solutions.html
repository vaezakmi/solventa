Our Solutions

Smartboards & Interactive Kits

Turn any projected surface into a dynamic interactive touch screen with our innovative Smarttouch kits designed to spark collaboration, boost engagement and transform every classroom into an immersive learning experience.

Projectors

Our DLP and android powered projectors are designed for dynamic, large-scale classroom presentations, ensuring every student can clearly see and engage with the lesson content.

Laptops, Desktops & Chromebooks

A range of student-focused computing devices, including powerful laptops for digital learning, high-performance desktops for labs and STEM classes, and lightweight Chromebooks for affordable, collaborative 1-to-1 programs.

Teacher Capacity Building

We offer comprehensive ICT training and professional development to empower teachers with the skills to effectively integrate technology into their lessons. Our dedicated support team provides ongoing guidance to ensure educators stay current with the latest trends and make the most of our solutions in the classroom.

Coding & Robotics

Our coding and robotics programs introduce students to the fundamentals of programming and engineering through hands-on projects. We provide the tools, resources, and training to help schools implement engaging STEM curricula that prepare students for the future.
